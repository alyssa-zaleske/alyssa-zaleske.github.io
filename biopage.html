Biography

 

Welcome to my website, my name is Alyssa Zaleske. I am a 22 year old college student majoring in digital design. I have two chiweenies, Luna and Nova, and am currently 6 months pregnant with my first baby. Some of my favorite things are Christmas, pumpkins, and Mexican food. Christmas is my favorite holiday; the weather and smells just bring joy to my life. I hate the hot weather but can not really do much about that. I currently live in Navarre with my parents until my baby girl comes and then my fiance and I will be moving into our own place. I enjoy designing things, because it allows me to dump all of the ideas I have in my head into something beautiful that can have the potential to move people.
